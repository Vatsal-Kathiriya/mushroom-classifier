Safety Guidelines

Critical Safety Information:

  • Never eat wild mushrooms based solely on AI predictions
  • Always consult with expert mycologists for identification
  • Use multiple identification methods and field guides
  • When in doubt, do not consume
  • This tool is for educational purposes only

Key Identification Features

Cap Shape Examine the overall shape of the mushroom cap
Cap Color Note the primary color and any color variations
Gill Color Check the color of the gills underneath the cap
Bruising/Bleeding Observe if the mushroom changes color when cut or bruised
Habitat Consider the environment where the mushroom was found
Season Note the time of year when the mushroom was found
